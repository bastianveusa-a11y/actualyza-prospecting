Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Actualyza ("we", "our", or "us") operates an AI-powered business intelligence and outreach platform for the healthcare and wellness industry. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our platform and our use of the Meta Ads Library API.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two categories of information:

  • Business information: Publicly available data about businesses, including business names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and publicly visible advertising activity on Meta platforms (via the Meta Ads Library API).
  • Contact information: Business email addresses found on publicly accessible business websites, used solely for B2B outreach purposes.

We do not collect personal information from individuals, end consumers, or patients.

2. How We Use Meta Ads Library Data

We use the Meta Ads Library API to access publicly visible advertising data for businesses operating in the dental, aesthetic, medical spa, and wellness industries in the United States. Specifically, we use this data to:

  • Identify businesses that are actively investing in digital marketing on Meta platforms.
  • Assess advertising activity levels (active/inactive) to prioritize outreach.
  • Provide our internal team with context when preparing personalized B2B communications.

We access only data that is publicly available through the Meta Ads Library. We do not access, store, or process any private user data, ad performance metrics, or audience targeting information.

3. Data Retention

Business information collected through our platform is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. Data related to businesses that are no longer part of our active outreach is deleted or anonymized within 90 days.

We do not sell, rent, or share business data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect the data we process, including encrypted storage, access controls, and secure transmission protocols. Access to our platform is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — Ads Library API for publicly available advertising data.
  • Notion — Internal data storage and organization.
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery.
  • Anthropic — AI-powered content generation.

6. Your Rights

If you are a business owner and believe your business information has been incorrectly processed or you wish to request deletion of your data from our system, you may contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our platform after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
